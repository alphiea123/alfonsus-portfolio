"G-boy" : Arduino Powered Videogame Console

Photo 1: This is the finished "G-Boy", built in with 9 games, in a Laser Cut MDF wood Enclosure. From this project, I was able to learn 3D modeling for laser cutters, soldering skills, coding experience, and a stronger understanding of Circuits and Electricity.
Photo 2: Parts used on the PCB Include potentiometer (for brightness control), Pushbuttons for game controls, resistors, and an 16x2 8-bit LCD screen.
Photo 3: (Back of PCB) Here is the Arduino UNO board which is soldered to the LCD on the front side. This is how the games are coded and what they run from.
